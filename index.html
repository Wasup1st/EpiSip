The Reality of Anaphylaxis

Millions are at risk, yet carrying an EpiPen daily remains a frustrating challenge. When seconds matter, accessibility is everything.

Buried in bags or purses, finding an auto-injector during an allergic reaction wastes precious time.

29.6%

What's Needed?

A better way to carry EpiPens that is accessible, affordable, and highly practical. Something you already bring with you every single day.

Our Solution

Hidden in plain sight.

EpiSip is slightly thicker than a standard insulated bottle, maintaining the same height profile. It keeps your water cold and your EpiPen secure, temperature-regulated, and immediately accessible.

Vacuum Insulated

Stainless steel outer bottle with vacuum insulation protects the EpiPen from extreme temperatures while keeping drinks cold.

Secure Storage Compartment

A dedicated, sealed central chamber perfectly fits a standard epinephrine auto-injector, keeping it safe from water and damage.

Rapid Access Bottom Cap

The screwable bottom cap allows for immediate extraction of the EpiPen in under 3 seconds during a panic situation.
